ALBUM COVERS

"BEHIND EVERY GREAT ALBUM IS A STRIKING ALBUM COVER"

Album covers have always been a thing for me. Since I was young and got Daft Punk's "Discovery" album, I was fascinated with cover-art. The thing that interested me, was that it almost made the music a little bit better, and vice versa. So when I became a bit older, my fascination was made into a passion. I got Photoshop and started to have a little fun. My friends also started making music, which meant that I could train as much as I liked. I feel like the graphic work means a lot, not just only in music, but everywhere. You can tell the correct story in any universe if doing right, and certainly also the other way around. That's why it is so important to make the perfect graphics for music, and businesses.
